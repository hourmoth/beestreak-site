BeeStreak Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

BeeStreak ("the App") is developed by Barry Auer. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

BeeStreak does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use.

Data Storage

All data you create in BeeStreak — including your streaks, completion dates, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

BeeStreak does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. The App does not track your usage or behavior in any way.

Third-Party Services

BeeStreak uses Apple's StoreKit framework solely to process in-app purchases. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer does not have access to your payment information.

Notifications

BeeStreak may send local notifications to remind you to check your streaks, but only if you explicitly enable this feature. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent through any external server.

Children's Privacy

BeeStreak does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Since no data is collected, changes are unlikely.
