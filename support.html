Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track a habit?

Tap the + button on the main screen to create a new streak. Then tap on the streak to open the calendar view, where you can tap any day to mark it as complete.

What happens if I miss a day?

Your current streak resets to zero, but your best streak is saved. You can always start building again — your previous completions remain on the calendar.

Can I mark a past day as complete?

Yes! Tap on any past day in the calendar to mark or unmark it.

How many streaks can I track?

You can track up to 3 streaks for free. A one-time purchase unlocks unlimited streaks — no subscriptions.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the upgrade screen and tap "Restore Purchase." Your unlock will be restored automatically if you purchased it with the same Apple ID.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data will be lost. We recommend keeping regular device backups through iCloud or your computer.

Does BeeStreak collect my data?

No. BeeStreak does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
